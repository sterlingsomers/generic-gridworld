There are 5 possible movements in this game for both player and adversary.
The adversary is limited to these moves only.
In the description below, 'player' refers to either you or the adversary.

•Up: Moves player up one empty space.

•Down: Moves player down one empty space.

•Left: Moves player left one empty space.

•Right: Moves player right one empty space.

•Stay Still: During this turn, the player does not move.

During gameplay you use the mouse to click on any of the buttons.
