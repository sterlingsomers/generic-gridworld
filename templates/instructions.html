Below is an image of the game arena. Each element in the game is described.

•Orange square is contolled by you, the player.

•Green square is the goal square, it does not move.

•Red square is the adversary. It is controlled by an AI.

•Gray square is empty space. Players are free to move to an adjacent empty space.

•Black square is an obstacle. Players cannot move into adjacent obstacles.
